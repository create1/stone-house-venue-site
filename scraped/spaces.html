Venue

spaces

Parlour

A mesmerizing bar and exquisite cocktails await you in a casual and riveting space downstairs.

Cocktail service for up to 100 or connect to the Showroom and host up to 200 guests.

The lounge

Offering the finest craft cocktails, plush leather seating, and gorgeous wood tables. Our rustic barn door allows for a private experience in this majestic room that will leave your guests in awe.

Table service for up to 50 guests, or combine it with the adjacent Dining Room to accommodate table service for up to 150 people.

The Great Hall

Stone walls, timber beams and elegant lighting set the backdrop for your group's formal dining experience.

Seats up to 100 guests at our rustic wooden tables.

The courtyard

Perfect for an outdoor wedding ceremony and al fresco dining. Framed by romantic stone walls, an intimate wine cavern, and a picturesque brick stairway, this space nestled against the backdrop of Nevada City's nature is the ideal setting for a Gold Country event you'll never forget.

Seating up to 130 for a ceremony and 100 for seated dinner service.

The cavern

Get creative in the cave. Built in the late 1800's as a brewery, you can make it your wedding hideaway, invite your guests in for wine and whiskey tastings, or host a small dinner. 

Fits up to 30 guests.

The Showroom

Featuring a state-of-the-art sound and lighting system and wooden dance floor, there is no better place in the Sierra Foothills to dance the night away.

Invite up to to 300 guests for cocktails and dancing. Or, We'll set up a dinner for 50 stage-side.

The Suite

Our 3 bedroom penthouse suite is a perfect place to get accommodate your daytime relaxation and overnight stay. Equipped with a steam shower, copper tub and kitchen for your every needs.

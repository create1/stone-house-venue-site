Celebrate With us!

Stone House is a historic gathering place in the heart of Nevada City, made for celebrations of all kinds. Start with cocktails in the Parlour or a quiet toast in the Cavern. Host a long-table dinner in the Great Hall or exchange vows under the open sky in the Courtyard. Let the night continue with live music and dancing in the Showroom.

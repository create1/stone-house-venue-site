Your Dream Wedding

Weddings at Stone House

Nevada City's Premier Wedding Venue

Say "I Do" in Historic Elegance

A Gold Country Wedding Destination

Stone House is Nevada City's premier wedding venue, where historic charm meets modern sophistication. Exchange vows under the open sky in our romantic Courtyard, celebrate with cocktails in the intimate Parlour, and dine with your loved ones in the majestic Great Hall.

From the ceremony to the last dance, every moment of your wedding day will unfold in breathtaking spaces designed for celebration. Our venue can accommodate intimate elopements to grand receptions for up to 300 guests.

Your Complete Wedding Experience

From ceremony to reception, we have everything you need

Ceremony & Vows

Exchange vows in our romantic outdoor Courtyard, surrounded by historic stone walls and natural beauty. Our versatile spaces accommodate ceremonies of any size, from intimate elopements to grand celebrations.

Reception & Dining

Host your wedding reception in the elegant Great Hall with timber beams and stunning architecture. Our professional catering team creates custom menus featuring locally sourced, farm-to-table cuisine.

Dance & Celebration

Dance the night away in our state-of-the-art Showroom with professional sound and lighting. The Suite provides luxury accommodations for the wedding party to prepare and stay overnight.

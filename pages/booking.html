The Stone House Experience

"A true historical masterpiece located in the heart of downtown Nevada City. A perfect blend of luxurious charm draped in enchanting ivy on stone, with access to the natural beauty of the Sierras, all walking distance from the finest shops, fare, and hotels."

We offer the highest quality options in one of the unique historic masterpieces in the downtown area of Nevada City. Walking distance from the National Hotel, eclectic clothing stores, and delicious food, you and your guests will make unforgettable memories together.

Enjoy the ease and beauty of the surrounding nature trails and activities. Feel free to book a tour and experience the magic of our ceremony patio and the enchantment of being surrounded by ivy and stone.

Luxury Couples Suite

Enjoy your big day in our new luxury couples suite on the top floor, including amenities like a large living room, open floor plan, gorgeous stoneware, a primary suite, a queen bedroom, and a luxury steam shower for two.

ADA Accessibility

Everything on our bottom floor is arranged to accommodate guests in wheelchairs. Our bathrooms on the bottom floor can be accessed. Please note: we do not have an elevator to the upper spaces.

Spaces Made for Celebration

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, Stone House offers a variety of unique spaces to suit your event needs. Each room tells its own story with historic charm and modern amenities.

Cocktails & Gathering

The Parlour

Capacity: Up to 100 Guests (Cocktails) | 200 with Showroom

A mesmerizing bar and exquisite cocktails await you in a casual and riveting space downstairs. Perfect for pre-event cocktails or standalone gatherings.

The Parlour features exposed brick, intimate lighting, and a full-service bar that sets the perfect atmosphere for mingling and celebration.

Intimate Dining

The Lounge

Capacity: Up to 50 Guests | 150 with Dining Room

Offering the finest craft cocktails, plush leather seating, and gorgeous wood tables. Our rustic barn door allows for a private experience in this majestic room.

The Lounge is perfect for intimate dinners, wine tastings, or as an exclusive cocktail space for your larger event.

Formal Dining

The Great Hall

Capacity: Up to 100 Guests

Stone walls, timber beams and elegant lighting set the backdrop for your group's formal dining experience at rustic wooden tables.

This stunning space embodies the historic character of Stone House with its soaring ceilings and architectural details that create an unforgettable ambiance.

Outdoor Elegance

The Courtyard

Ceremony: 130 Guests | Seated Dinner: 100 Guests

Perfect for an outdoor wedding ceremony and al fresco dining. Framed by romantic stone walls, an intimate wine cavern, and a picturesque brick stairway.

This space, nestled against the backdrop of Nevada City's natural beauty, is the ideal setting for a Gold Country event you'll never forget.

Historic Wine Cave

The Cavern

Capacity: Up to 30 Guests

Get creative in the cave. Built in the late 1800's as a brewery, this unique space offers endless possibilities.

Make it your wedding hideaway, host intimate wine and whiskey tastings, or arrange a small private dinner in this one-of-a-kind underground venue.

Dance & Entertainment

The Showroom

Dancing: 300 Guests | Dinner: 50 Guests

Featuring a state-of-the-art sound and lighting system and wooden dance floor, there is no better place in the Sierra Foothills to dance the night away.

Perfect for wedding receptions, corporate events with entertainment, or as Nevada City's premier live music venue with professional acoustics and atmosphere.

Luxury Accommodations

The Suite

3 Bedroom Penthouse

Our 3 bedroom penthouse suite is a perfect place to accommodate your daytime relaxation and overnight stay.

Equipped with a steam shower, copper tub, and full kitchen, the Suite provides luxurious comfort for wedding parties, extended guests, or anyone wanting the full Stone House experience.

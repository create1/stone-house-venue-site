Welcome to

A Historic Nevada City Landmark

Stone House is a historic gathering place in the heart of Nevada City, made for celebrations of all kinds. Built in the late 1800s, our venue combines the charm of Gold Country heritage with modern amenities to create an unforgettable experience for you and your guests.

From intimate gatherings in our wine cavern to grand celebrations in our showroom, each of our unique spaces offers its own character and ambiance, perfect for weddings, corporate events, live music, and memorable occasions.

Weddings

Create your dream wedding in our historic venue with multiple ceremony and reception spaces to choose from.

Live Music

Experience world-class entertainment in our state-of-the-art showroom with superior acoustics and lighting.

Corporate Events

Host impressive business gatherings in a unique setting that will leave a lasting impression.

Our Unique Spaces

Each space at Stone House offers its own distinctive character

The Courtyard

Capacity: 100-130 Guests

Perfect for outdoor wedding ceremonies and al fresco dining. Framed by romantic stone walls and an intimate wine cavern.

The Great Hall

Capacity: Up to 100 Guests

Stone walls, timber beams and elegant lighting set the backdrop for your formal dining experience.

The Showroom

Capacity: Up to 300 Guests

State-of-the-art sound and lighting system with a wooden dance floor. The perfect place to dance the night away.

The Cavern

Capacity: Up to 30 Guests

Built in the late 1800s as a brewery. Perfect for intimate wine tastings and small gatherings.
